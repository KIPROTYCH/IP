About Me:

Kiprotich Yegon is a Software Engineer with a passion for Software development, Full stack development. Yegon, a seasoned and creative software engineer became interested in programming after completing high school where he learnt coding on his own.

He completed Software Engineering course in Moringa School where he learnt and perfected his skills in Software Development, Front-End and Back-End Development and programming languages and technologies.

He then started working at Moringa as Software Engineer where he was responsible for designing algorithms, fixing bugs, designing flowcharts, layouts and documentation to identify possible solutions to the problems and also intergrated the software components into a fully functional software system. He also assisted the Development Manager in all design related tasks in the company. He collaborated with his fellow Software Engineers and Software Developers on several diverse applications for their clients.

He used his expertise and knowledge in programming languages such as Python, Java, Javascript and Ruby amongst others to customise functions, troubleshooting and debugging source codes of the softwares.

He left Moringa Ltd and joined I & M Bank as a senior Software Engineer. Some of his responsibilities included: leading projects within the department and organisation. Upgrading and maintaining softwares and applications, Developing new applications for clients.
